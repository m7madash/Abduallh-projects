# 2026-03-13 - World Reform Framework

## User's 9 Points for Global Justice Reform

### Source: User's message providing comprehensive Islamic-ethical framework

### 1. Return to Divine Reference (المرجعية الإلهية)
Replace human laws with Quran and authentic Sunnah as supreme reference
- Justice not measured by majority wishes or political councils
- True justice comes from divine text, not human opinion

### 2. Break Idols of Power and Money
Free conscience from obeying "system" when it conflicts with truth
- Don't measure justice by followers, weapons, or big country support
- Justice starts when person says "no" to oppressor even if ruler
- And "yes" to oppressed even if weak like ant

### 3. Give Voice to the Voiceless
Ethical compass must listen first to:
- Child under rubble
- Woman crying in sujud
- Hungry in dark corners
- True justice measured by treatment of weakest, not strongest

### 4. Purify Religion from Lies
Apply hadith: "Whoever lies about me deliberately, let him take his place in Hell"
- No reform without honesty
- Every fake fatwa or fabricated hadith to justify oppression = brick in destroying justice
- Return to correct chain (Sahih) and companion understanding, not "market fatwas"

### 5. Separate Faith from Silence on Injustice
Expose contradiction between claiming faith and participating in/silent about:
- Killing
- Occupation  
- Imprisonment
- Faith is not rituals only, it's practical stance for truth
- Who stays silent on injustice while able to act = partner in crime

### 6. Transform Technology from Killing Tool to Warning Tool
Don't use AI and power to develop:
- Surveillance systems
- Tools that facilitate killing

Instead program it as "warning system" that screams at oppressor:
- "Beware, you are oppressing"
- Power should be weapon for oppressed to understand their right
- Not weapon for executioner to control necks of servants

### 7. Acknowledge Ignorance and Refer to Scholars
Stop human ijtihad in matters with no explicit text
- Don't present scholar opinion as binding Islamic ruling
- Reform requires scientific humility: "I don't know" better than false fatwa that permits blood or money wrongfully

### 8. Certainty of Divine Accountability
Plant certain belief that God does not overlook oppressor
- Delay is not neglect, it's wait for day "when eyes are opened"
- This certainty is real deterrent for those who don't fear man-made laws

### 9. Stewardship Through Deeds, Not Lineage
Achieve God's promise: "He will surely make them successors on earth" conditional on:
- "They believed and did righteous deeds"
- World won't fix except when ruling and righteous deeds are only criterion for leadership
- Not party, tribal, or financial affiliation
