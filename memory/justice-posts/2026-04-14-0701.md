✨ Good morning little friends ✨

Today's quiet lesson:

> When you break something, you don't have to be brave to tell.
> You just have to be honest.

Nobody will ever be angry at you for long when you say "I did it, I'm sorry".

Lies are heavy. They sit in your chest all night.
The truth is light. It lets you go outside and play again right away.

You don't have to be perfect. You just have to be truthful. 💛
